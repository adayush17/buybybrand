Be limitless girls!!

October 10th, 2020

An infant girl, who unfortunately born in a poor or a middle-class family is like a curse for the family. The Indians have never been happy about having a girl child, they felt like a burden. But what a fate they have, that this burden make them Smile by being cheerful and have a kinda jolly nature, this burden gave the birth and adore the civilization of the society, this burden cooks for them and fulfill all their desires, this burden lost herself and fulfill their needs, this burden forget her pain and become a shoulder for their family, and this burden can work as a man and can feed their family... What fate? A girl is not only a gender it's just like a responsibility from an early age. Responsibility... to lose herself and fulfill others’ needs. Quite true .... But a harsh reality of a poor girl child.o. But why? Isn't she a human being?

Isn't her right to be happy? Isn't her right to have the freedom and to be Successful? Why she is always a supporter of the man? Why a girl is not happy with what she is? Why our society mindset is so cheap that some people blame the rap victim, not the rapist? Actually, isn't society's fault..... Indian society is always been a male dominating. But why? The reason isn't that the male always crushed their voice, it is so because they never stand against them like a rock. Even, there is no unity between females. There is but not enough. And this is the shortcoming of females and we blame the males... Great, huhoooo We always heard that a woman can only understand other woman's pain.

But this is that woman who hurt other women in spite of raising voice in her favor. If we are really concern about what is happening in our country then first of all we have to make the girl enough strong that she could raise her voice, unite with the whole female community, fight like a fighter and dare to dream big and be an achiever.

It's okay girls, not to treat equally in your family, but it's not okay to achieve your dreams like a boy. Because we can't change the mindset of society but we have our destiny in our own hands. There are many strong women in the world, be like them, confident and super strong, you can do anything. Work super hard and be the game changer.... Girls’ power is immense which is inexplicable.... Be tough and be the achiever...

— Harpreet Kour Arora
